About us.

"As a new business, cocoa forest already has been receiving a generous amount of feedback from customers. we have our very own unique theme and style. In addition to that, we serve specialty coffee and our own creation of a variety interesting blended drinks and sweet treats. we strive to make each visit a memorable experience for their customers. They are also vegan friendly, which could be a great factor to make our shop stronger than many other coffee shops."

cocoa forest provides its customers with a unique experience that isnt duplicated anywhere else. cocoa forest, unlike its competition, created an environment that caters to every individual with everyones favorite coffee beverages and some new, fun, creative creations that are unique such as the Frankenstein and Cookies & Scream blended drinks. With our off-the-wall personality and our cater-to-all styled beverage menu, we have created a brand for itself that is sure to stand above the competition. Furthermore, as a small coffee shop, it could be easier to maintain the business. we have a high potential to expand to more locations with different radical themes to attract more diverse types of people with different personalities and interests. and young adults are starting to be more concerned about healthier options when it comes to choosing the right food and drink to consume.
